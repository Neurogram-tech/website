Clinical Neurophysiology

Beyond the conventional features to inspect an exam, Neurogram has an entire sector dedicated specifically to clinical neurophysiology. With a 100% online interface, the doctor can upload the EEG of many file types (.cnt, .eeg, .set, .edf…), visualize it from any device with internet access and share the EEG and their comments with other doctors (HIPAA compliant)!

Research Neurophysiology

An entire interface exclusively for research, full of features for pre-processing, data visualization and signal analysis that goes beyond the common EEG analysis methods! We included 3 different filter types, ICA, a 3D voxel visualization and 5 different connectivity analysis methods all in one place!
